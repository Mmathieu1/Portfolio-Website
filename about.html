About Me.

I'm Middy and I've been immersed in the art, design, communication, and animal welfare for a combined 14 years.

I’m a UX/UI designer with a strong illustration, graphic design, and customer service background. As a freelance illustrator and graphic designer, I am highly self-motivated and understand how to adapt to sudden changes as they arise.

I have personally prepared for production several children’s books from scratch and enjoy working with my hands to create something into existence. Working in print production I have gained skills that are vital to user-centered designs such as product attractiveness, readability, accessibility, and most importantly an enjoyable experience with a product through user testing.

Along with design I enjoy creating with my hands and have picked up nearly every hobby I have come across- and there are still so many to try! I'm very passionate when it comes to animals. I've held a PetCare Manager tittle at a retail store, worked as a kennel technician, and have fostered back to health over 40 kittens. I enjoy spending my extra time curling up on the couch with my two cats and dog while watching the latest Disney movie.
