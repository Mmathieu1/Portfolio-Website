Purrsuit

Scavenger hunt apps provide users with social stimulation alongside mental and some physical activity. The app needs to be simple yet intriguing enough to be able hold the attention for a group of multiple players for the time span of the game. App will need to be reliable where internet connection is scarce as to not disconnect the players. We know these features are important, when interviewing players and reviewing similar app reviews; the most common complaints being not able to play without internet connection and gameplay is too simple and repetitive-making the player bored. To make Purrsuit a Scavenger hunt to stand out from the competitors:

  • Making the ability to play in any sized group, including a single player.
  • Give the App a creative theme.mission to keep the user's attention through the entire game play.
  • Provide pre-made hunts as well as generic hunts that can be played anywhere.

Overview

Purrsuit is an app designed to be an interactive scavenger/treasure hunt that provides a fun and challenging activity for a wide range of players. (Extra points for cat lovers!) Purrsuit's fun theme will provide entertainment for many played games to come!

Purpose & Context

Purrsuit is a sinlge person project completed as a part of CareerFoundry's Certified UX Design Immersion Course. During this course I was introduced and dove into the full UX process from Research, Interviews to Wireframes and Mockups. The goal being able to apply these skills to build a mobil application in course and our in real world projects.
